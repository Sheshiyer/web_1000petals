Welcome to The Lucia No03 .

Hypnagogic light machine

Sit or lie down with your eyes closed, listen to some soothing music and let the journey begin!!! Yes it’s as simple as that to experience what meditators experience after several years of meditation practice. The eight low UV emission LED lights and the centre Halogen are kept from you at a 40CM to 50CM distance and you are welcomed into the session with a 1000 Petals’ signature HRV breathwork pattern. The HRV breathwork pattern makes you relax completely gets you in tune with your mind and body. And then… you experience a journey that you have never ever experienced before…

White Light then passes through your closed eyelids, reaches the pineal gland which is the white light receptor of the brain and starts decalcifying the same with the photonic energy particles of Light itself. The decalcification and the activation of the pineal gland via the white light stimulation creates a very unique visual experience which is entirely the result of your consciousness. A typical session on the Lucia no03 lasts from 30 Minutes to 55 Minutes and cannot be described in a specific way because of its myriad experiential array of the experience itself.

Hypnagogia is the magical space between wakefulness and sleep; powerful for deep relaxation, cosmic journeying, lucid dreaming, creative visioning, deepening meditation practice and much more. Consciously conceived, developed & created in Alps of Austria by Dr. Engelbert Winkler (psychiatrist/psychologist) & Dr. Dirk Proeckl (medical neurologist/psychologist). Hypnagogia is the state of consciousness between waking and sleep. This special state with the aid of different techniques helps find answers and gain new impetus. Much has been researched & published about hypnagogic state and lucid dreaming, yet it is always the actual experience of it that is the centre of attention.
